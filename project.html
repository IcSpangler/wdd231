Site Plan: CineFácil – Movie Recommendations Made Easy

Site Name

CineFácil — The name combines “Cine” (cinema) and “Fácil” (easy), representing the site’s goal of making movie discovery quick and simple for everyone. Optional domain availability: cinefacil.com

Site Purpose

CineFácil will help users find great movies based on their mood or favorite genres. It will feature curated movie recommendations, genre galleries, and a suggestion form where visitors can share their own movie picks. The goal is to make movie discovery fast, fun, and personalized.

Scenarios

  • “I’m in the mood for a comedy tonight. What should I watch?”
  • “I love suspense movies. Can I see a list of the best ones?”
  • “Can I suggest my favorite movie for others to see?”

Color Schema

Primary Color: #C94277 – Used for headings, buttons, and highlights.

Secondary Color: #2F0A28 – Used for navigation, footers, and accents.

Background: #FFFFFF – White for clean readability.

Typography

Main Font: Poppins – Used for both headings and body text for a clean, modern look.
