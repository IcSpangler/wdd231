Site Plan: CineFácil – Movie Recommendations Made Easy

By Isabelle C Spangler

Site Name

CineFácil – The name combines “Cine” (cinema) and “Fácil” (easy), representing the site’s goal of making movie discovery quick and simple for everyone.

Optional domain availability: cinefacil.com

Site Purpose

CineFácil helps users discover great movies based on their mood or favorite genres. Whether you want to laugh, cry, or feel the suspense, CineFácil makes finding the perfect movie simple and fun. It features curated movie recommendations, genre-based galleries, and a suggestion form for visitors to share their own favorites.

Scenarios

  • “I’m in the mood for a comedy tonight. What should I watch?”
  • “I love suspense movies. Can I see a list of the best ones?”
  • “Can I suggest my favorite movie for others to see?”

Color Schema

Primary Color: #C94277 – Used for headings, buttons, and highlights.

Secondary Color: #2F0A28 – Used for navigation, footers, and accents.

Background Color: #FFFFFF – White for clean readability.

Typography

Poppins: Used for headings and navigation items for a modern, clean look.

Merriweather: Used for body text for good readability and a friendly feel.
